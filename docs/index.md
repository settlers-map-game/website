## How it works

<div class="mechanics" markdown>

<div class="mechanics-card" markdown>

### Capture real-world hexes

<div class="map-embed map-embed--small">
  <iframe
    src="https://tma.settlers.top/onboarding-map?hideHeader=true&lat=41.38862118583738&lng=2.153336682819262&zoom=13"
    title="Settlers hex capture example"
    loading="lazy"
    allow="geolocation"
  ></iframe>
</div>

- Move to a real-world location and press **Settle** to claim the hex.
- Every hex can have only one owner at a time.
- Once captured, a hex is protected by a **1-hour cooldown** — no one can steal it immediately.

</div>

<div class="mechanics-card" markdown>

### Join clans, control territories

<div class="map-embed map-embed--small">
  <iframe
    src="https://tma.settlers.top/onboarding-map?hideHeader=true&lat=41.38862118583738&lng=2.153336682819262&zoom=12"
    title="Settlers clan territory example"
    loading="lazy"
    allow="geolocation"
  ></iframe>
</div>

- Team up with other players to control larger areas.
- Clan territory updates dynamically as members capture new hexes.
- Clan members can't capture hexes from each other, so coordination pays off.

</div>

<div class="mechanics-card" markdown>

### Earn points & climb the leaderboard

- Held hexes generate points automatically over time.
- Busier and more interesting locations usually give more points.
- The more territory you control, the faster you rise.

</div>

<div class="mechanics-card" markdown>

### Invite friends

- Share your clan invitation link to recruit teammates.
- Friends who join through your link become your referrals.
- Grow your clan and strengthen your territory together.

</div>

</div>
